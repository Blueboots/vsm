Voyager Golden Record T-Shirts
& other space program themed merch

Space program themed T-shirts featuring original designs inspired by the Golden Records carried by NASA's two Voyager probes. Each golden record contained encoded information about Earth, humanity, and its various cultures. The design featured on these T-shirts is the instructions engraved on the reverse of the golden record intended to show any alien intelligence encountering the disc how to decode the data. Launched in 1977, the Voyager satellites were sent into space from Cape Canaveral, Florida to explore the outer reaches of our solar system. On August 25, 2012, signals received from Voyager 1 indicated that it had become the first human-made object to enter interstellar space, traveling further than anyone, or anything, in history.

As well as this Golden Record design, a whole range of other T-shirts, mugs, phone cases, and other items are available globally, all featuring iconic images from NASA's many historic space missions launched over the past decades, such as the Apollo moon landings, the Pioneer probes, Skylab and many more. Each design comes in a variety of shirt styles (for men, women, and children), and different colors. Click on any design to see the whole range of variations.
